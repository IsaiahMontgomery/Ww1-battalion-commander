# Project Roadmap for WW1 Battalion Commander

## Phase 1: Initial Concept and Research (2026-02-15 to 2026-04-15)
- **Milestone 1.1**: Define project goals and objectives.
- **Milestone 1.2**: Conduct market research and competitor analysis.
- **Milestone 1.3**: Develop initial project scope and requirements.

## Phase 2: Design and Prototyping (2026-04-16 to 2026-06-30)
- **Milestone 2.1**: Create wireframes and user interfaces.
- **Milestone 2.2**: Develop a working prototype of the gameplay.
- **Milestone 2.3**: Gather feedback from initial user testing.

## Phase 3: Development Kickoff (2026-07-01 to 2026-10-15)
- **Milestone 3.1**: Establish development environment and repository.
- **Milestone 3.2**: Implement core gameplay mechanics.
- **Milestone 3.3**: Complete first playable version.

## Phase 4: Expanded Development (2026-10-16 to 2027-01-15)
- **Milestone 4.1**: Add additional features and content.
- **Milestone 4.2**: Conduct regular testing and iteration.
- **Milestone 4.3**: Prepare for beta launch.

## Phase 5: Beta Testing (2027-01-16 to 2027-04-15)
- **Milestone 5.1**: Release beta version to selected testers.
- **Milestone 5.2**: Collect and analyze user feedback.
- **Milestone 5.3**: Fix critical bugs and polish gameplay.

## Phase 6: Launch and Post-Launch (2027-04-16 onward)
- **Milestone 6.1**: Officially launch the game.
- **Milestone 6.2**: Monitor performance and fix post-launch issues.
- **Milestone 6.3**: Plan for future updates and expansions.
